About SLCT

SLCT is passionate about empowering the deaf and those with hearing challenges by providing innovative solutions for sign language translation. Our commitment to accessibility and inclusivity drives us to deliver exceptional services that facilitate seamless communication through sign language.

Our Services

Sign Language Classification

SLCT is dedicated to providing accurate and reliable sign language classification for various contexts and purposes. Our team ensures precise translation of sign language gestures into text, helping bridge communication gaps for the deaf and those with hearing impairments.

Translation from Images

At SLCT, we specialize in translating sign language from images, allowing our users to receive clear, written explanations of the signed content within their uploaded pictures. Our service aims to make sign language more accessible and understandable for everyone.
